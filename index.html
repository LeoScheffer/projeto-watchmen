THE WATCHMEN?

In a world of masked heroes and hidden agendas, even the protectors need watching.

Characters

rorschach

No one truly knows who Rorschach is. A shadow in the alleys, a whisper in the dark. Some say he’s just a man in a mask — others believe he’s justice itself, walking in flesh. His face never changes, yet it’s never the same. Always shifting… like the truth he seeks. He sees the world in black and white, good and evil — no in-between. And when the city sleeps, he watches. Waiting. Judging. Writing it all down in his journal. If you're guilty, pray he doesn't come knocking. Because once he finds you… there’s no mask to hide behind.

SILK SPECTRE II

She moves like a memory — graceful, untouchable, dangerous. They call her Silk Spectre, but that’s only a name. Beneath the silk and shadows is a woman torn between legacy and freedom. Raised in the glow of someone else’s spotlight, trained to be more than a symbol. She smiles for the cameras, but her fists speak louder. Some say she’s running from the past. Others say she’s carving a new future. One thing is certain: underestimate her, and you’ll learn that silk can cut deeper than steel.

DR. MANHATTAN

He is no longer man. Time bends around him — past, present, future, all at once. They call him Dr. Manhattan, but names mean little to a god. His skin glows like a star, his eyes see what no one else can. He walks among us, yet exists far beyond. Detached. Infinite. Unstoppable. Some fear him. Some worship him. But he feels nothing. Because when you’ve seen the end of everything… what meaning can there be in anything?

THE COMEDIAN

They laughed with him… until they realized the joke was on them. The Comedian — a man who wore war like a second skin and smiled through the chaos. To him, life was never about justice or peace — it was a cruel joke, and he was the only one in on the punchline. Brutal. Unfiltered. Unapologetic. He saw the world for what it was — broken, bleeding, absurd — and played his part with a grin and a loaded gun. Heroes hated him. Politicians used him. But in the end, maybe he was the only one who truly understood the joke.

The Plot...

  •

    The comedian's murder

    The Comedian's Murder was no random crime — it was a message. A powerful man thrown from his apartment window, silenced in a world he once mocked. To the public, he was a decorated hero. To those who knew him, a symbol of chaos wrapped in stars and stripes. But someone wanted him gone… someone who knew too much. Blood on the badge, shattered glass on the pavement — and behind it all, a secret that could unravel everything.

  •

    Rorschach investigates

    Rorschach Investigates is a descent into the shadows of a city rotting from the inside. With only his journal, instinct, and a mask that never blinks, Rorschach pieces together a conspiracy tied to the murder of the Comedian. Every clue pulls him deeper — into secrets the world was never meant to uncover. Justice isn't blind. It's watching… and it's wearing inkblots

  •

    The doomsday clock

    The Doomsday Clock is ticking. A symbol once used to warn the world… now a countdown to its end. Set by scientists, ignored by nations — each second closer to midnight echoes humanity’s failure to change. In the shadows, powers move. Heroes fall. Truth bends. As the hands creep forward, one question remains: is it too late to turn them back… or has the clock already struck?
